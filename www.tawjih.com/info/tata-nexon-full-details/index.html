Tata Nexon full details

March 31, 2025 March 31, 2025 Trending

The Tata Nexon is one of the most popular compact SUVs in India, offering a combination of modern features, safety, and powerful performance. It has made a significant impact in the sub-4-meter SUV segment and is available in both petrol and diesel variants. Here’s a comprehensive overview of the Tata Nexon:

Tata Nexon Overview

  • Launch Year : 2017 (Facelifted in 2020)
  • Price : The price range of the Tata Nexon starts around ₹8.30 Lakhs (ex-showroom) and goes up to ₹14.80 Lakhs (ex-showroom), depending on the variant.

Variants

The Tata Nexon comes in multiple variants to suit a wide range of buyers. The main variants include:

  • XE
  • XM
  • XT
  • XZ+
  • XZ+ (O)

Additionally, there are also electric versions (Nexon EV), which have their own specific variants like XM , XZ+ , and XZ+ LUX .

Engine Options

The Tata Nexon comes with both petrol and diesel engine options:

  1. Petrol Engine :
    • Engine Capacity : 1.2L Turbocharged Revotron Petrol
    • Power : 120 PS (88.2 kW) @ 5500 rpm
    • Torque : 170 Nm @ 1750-4000 rpm
    • Transmission : 6-speed manual or 6-speed automatic
    • Fuel Efficiency : Around 17.5 – 18.0 km/l (manual) and 16 – 16.5 km/l (automatic)
  2. Diesel Engine :
    • Engine Capacity : 1.5L Turbocharged Revotorq Diesel
    • Power : 110 PS (81.5 kW) @ 3750 rpm
    • Torque : 260 Nm @ 1500-2750 rpm
    • Transmission : 6-speed manual or 6-speed automatic
    • Fuel Efficiency : Around 21 – 22 km/l (manual) and 19.5 – 20.5 km/l (automatic)

Dimensions

  • Length : 3993 mm
  • Width : 1811 mm
  • Height : 1606 mm
  • Wheelbase : 2600 mm
  • Ground Clearance : 209 mm (unladen)
  • Boot Space : 350 liters

Features

The Tata Nexon comes packed with features that cater to both comfort and convenience. Here are some key features:

Exterior Features :

  • LED DRLs (Daytime Running Lights)
  • Tri-arrow LED Tail-lamps
  • Bold front grille
  • 16-inch alloy wheels
  • Roof rails
  • Front & rear skid plates
  • Projector headlamps

Interior Features :

  • 7-inch touchscreen infotainment system (Tata’s Harman unit)
  • Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility
  • Automatic climate control
  • Rear AC vents
  • 6-way adjustable driver seat
  • Leatherette upholstery (higher variants)
  • Push-button start/stop
  • Cruise control
  • Digital instrument cluster

Safety Features :

  • 5-star Global NCAP rating for safety
  • Dual front airbags
  • ABS with EBD
  • Cornering stability control
  • Traction control system
  • Electronic Stability Program (ESP)
  • Rear parking sensors with camera
  • ISOFIX child seat mounts
  • Front and rear fog lamps
  • Rear defogger

Tata Nexon EV (Electric Version)

Tata also offers an electric version of the Nexon, the Tata Nexon EV , which has become quite popular in India due to its practicality and performance.

Tata Nexon EV Specifications :

  • Battery Capacity : 30.2 kWh (Standard) or 40.5 kWh (Long Range)
  • Range : 312 km (on a full charge with the 40.5 kWh battery) as per ARAI certification
  • Power : 129 PS (95 kW)
  • Torque : 245 Nm
  • Charging : 0-80% in 60 minutes with a fast charger, 0-100% in about 8 hours with a standard charger
  • Variants : XM, XZ+, XZ+ LUX

Price Range (Ex-Showroom)

  • Petrol Variants : ₹8.30 Lakhs to ₹12.80 Lakhs
  • Diesel Variants : ₹9.50 Lakhs to ₹13.50 Lakhs
  • Tata Nexon EV : ₹14.99 Lakhs to ₹18.50 Lakhs (depending on the variant)

Prices may vary depending on location, dealer, and optional features. Please check with a local dealer for the most up-to-date pricing.

Key Pros of Tata Nexon :

  • Great safety features : It comes with a 5-star safety rating from Global NCAP.
  • Stylish design : Modern design with SUV-like appeal.
  • Feature-rich cabin : Plenty of tech and comfort features.
  • Spacious : Good cabin space for a family car.
  • Powerful and efficient engines : Offers solid performance, especially in the turbocharged variants.

Key Cons of Tata Nexon :

  • Interior plastics : Some users report that the interior plastics could feel more premium.
  • Ride quality : Although it offers decent comfort, the ride can be a bit firm at times.
